# GDTask

For information, see https://github.com/ProgrammerOnCoffee/GDTask.


## Basic Usage

- To create a new task, click the new button at the bottom of the window.
By default, the new task will be opened immediately so you can quickly edit it.

- To open tasks and edit their text, add notes, and more, right-click them.

- To change a task's color, either left- or right-click the colored area on its
left side or open it and click the colored bar at the top of the window.

- To delete tasks, either hold shift and right-click them or open them and press
the delete button.

- To rearrange tasks, left-click and drag them up and down.
